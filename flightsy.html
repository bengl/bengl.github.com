Flightsy

Flightsy is an Android app for creating tweetable itineraries. That is, itineraries of the form "SFO ✈ JFK ✈ LHR", etc.

At the moment, it's not quite ready for general use, so I've made it available here rather than the Android Market for now. Therefore, you'll need to enable non-Market apps.

For simplicity's sake, this will currently only run on Android 4.0+.

Download Flightsy to your Android phone right now!
